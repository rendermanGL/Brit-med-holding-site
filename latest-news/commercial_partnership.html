COMMERCIAL PARTNERSHIP AGREEMENT WITH EXCLUSIVELY EASTBOURNE

Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com sign a commercial partnership agreement with serviced accommodation provider, Exclusively Eastbourne.

Leading digital healthcare provider, Hextransforma Healthcare Limited is delighted to announce the signing of a commercial partnership agreement for the provision of exclusive, serviced, self-catering accommodation in East Sussex for its patient mobility platform Brit-Med.com.

Exclusively Eastbourne and the patient mobility platform Brit-Med.com have entered into a commercial partnership through which Exclusively Eastbourne’s portfolio of high-end properties will be integrated into the first global patient mobility platform of its kind.

This move will allow Brit-Med.com patients and their families access to book high-quality, self-catering properties in the beautiful East Sussex (United Kingdom) countryside or by its idyllic stunning beaches.

Jean-Luc Bressard CEO, Hextransforma Healthcare added: “Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com understand that patients travelling for healthcare reasons require access to high-quality accommodation for themselves and their families, following their procedures and release from hospital. That is why we are partnering with trusted providers and organisations such as Exclusively Eastbourne, as we recognise that our patients want and deserve the very best environment for their convalescence and stay in the United Kingdom.

We are working closely with our partners and local tourist offices across the United Kingdom to revive the ‘Spa town history’ of many historic UK Spa Towns, where many British people during the 17th, 18th and 19th centuries used to travel for health reasons.

The many valid reasons that people used to travel to these Spa Towns still remain present and compelling today and we want our overseas patients to benefit from the same idyllic and rejuvenating properties offered by many of our beautiful British towns and cities”.

Sarah Stanger, Director of Exclusively Eastbourne added: “We are delighted to be involved with such an exciting new initiative and look forward to introducing Brit-Med clients to the delights of our glorious countryside and beaches. This is the perfect area for recuperation and rejuvenation and with our hand-picked holiday homes ranging in size from one-bedroom properties to family-sized houses, patients will be able to find the perfect base for relaxation.”
