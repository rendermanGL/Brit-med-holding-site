ANDREW SKYRME FRCS APPOINTED CHIEF MEDICAL OFFICER

Andrew Skyrme FRCS appointed Chief Medical Officer, Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com and Chair of the Medical Advisory Board.

Leading digital healthcare provider, Hextransforma Healthcare Limited is delighted to announce the appointment of Andrew Skyrme as Chief Medical Officer and Chair of the medical Advisory Board.

Andrew Skyrme brings to the board 30 years of medical and leadership experience. In addition to Andrew’s position with the company, Andrew retains his private medical practice and NHS responsibilities which lie principally in lower limb surgery.

Andrew specialises specifically in complex hip surgery including revision hip surgery and uncemented and cemented replacements. stem, the Exeter stem and also the Birmingham resurfacing.

As Chair, Andrew assumes full responsibility for the leadership of the Medical Advisory Board which approves on-boarding of healthcare provides onto our panel and scrutinises the performance of all Healthcare Providers that work with the company.

Andrew Skyrme added: “Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com are leading the way in Digital Healthcare transformation globally and will very quickly establish themselves not only as the UK leader, but a truly global leader in patient mobility. I am very excited to be joining such an innovative, dynamic and driven team. We have a tremendous opportunity to grow the business and to diversify into new sectors such as digital medicine and telemetric data. I am also pleased to join an Executive team focussed on delivering improved social outcomes in all the markets in which we operate.

In a fast moving UK digital healthcare market, we at Hextransforma Healthcare and brit-med.com are in an excellent position to capitalise on the growth opportunities presented.

Hextransforma Healthcare’s brand, values, diversity and delivery models are facets I respect and I am excited to be on board and part of a great leadership team that will take the company to the next level.”
