MARC PACIFICO FRCS (PLAST.) APPOINTED NON-EXECUTIVE DIRECTOR

Marc Pacifico FRCS (Plast.) Non-Executive Director, Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com and member of the Medical Advisory Board.

Leading digital healthcare provider, Hextransforma Healthcare Limited is delighted to announce the appointment of Marc Pacifico as Non-Executive Director and member of the Medical Advisory Board.

Marc Pacifico brings to our board extensive medical experience. Marc is an eminent plastic surgeon and winner of the Ian McGregor “gold” medal for coming top in the United Kingdom in the FRCS (Plast.) exam.

Marc graduated from St Bartholomew’s Hospital Medical College and undertook his surgical training in a number of prestigious hospitals in London. His specialist plastic surgery training started at Charing Cross Hospital, London and from there he spent 5-years at Mount Vernon Hospital, Northwood, during which time he completed his doctorate on improving outcomes in melanoma. Subsequently, he trained at The Royal Free Hospital, London and Queen Victoria Hospital, East Grinstead, where he worked as a Consultant Plastic Surgeon for 8-years.

Marc with work closely with Andrew Skyrme (Chair, Medical Advisory Board) in the Medical Advisory Board with a particular focus on the Plastic Surgeon Healthcare Providers on our Panel.

The Medical Advisory Board approves the on-boarding of healthcare provides onto our panel and scrutinises the performance of all Healthcare Providers that work with the company.

Marc Pacifico added: “Hextransforma Healthcare and Brit-Med.com are providing innovative mechanisms through which plastic surgery patients can compare the profiles of clinicians and healthcare providers as well as the prices being offered for procedures.

Price transparency and quality of care and outcomes are a priority for us as a business and I look forward not only contributing to the improvement of outcomes of all patients travelling for healthcare reasons but also to being part of a team whose solutions will make a positive impact on the global industry as a whole.
